01 — About
Who We Are

NEXUS.LAUNCH is the heartbeat of our campus startup ecosystem — a collective of ambitious minds who believe that building the future shouldn't wait until after graduation.

We're a student-run startup club that bridges the gap between classroom theory and real-world execution. From first-time founders to seasoned side-hustle veterans, our community is your launchpad.

We operate on one core belief: the best time to start is right now. Our programs, mentors, and network exist to remove every excuse standing between you and your first venture.

// Founder & President

Alex started NEXUS.LAUNCH from a dorm room with nothing but a whiteboard and a obsessive conviction that campus was sleeping on its own talent. Three years later, that conviction has become a movement.

Before founding NEXUS, Rachit built and sold two micro-SaaS products before age 20, interned at a Series B startup in Berlin, and turned down a cushy corporate offer to bet everything on the community. No regrets.

The mission is simple: create the environment Alex wished had existed — one where every student with a spark finds the fuel, framework, and fellow founders to ignite it.

03 — Culture
How We
Operate

We don't do passive membership. NEXUS culture is built on radical participation, honest feedback, and a collective refusal to stay comfortable. If you're in — you're all in.

⚡
Bias Toward Action

Ideas are cheap. Shipping is sacred. We celebrate done over perfect, iteration over paralysis, and try over wait. If it can be built in a weekend — build it this weekend.

🔥
Radical Honesty

Feedback here isn't padded with pleasantries. We give it straight because we respect each other's time and ambitions. Growth lives in discomfort.

🌐
Community First

Your win is our win. We co-promote, co-invest, and co-build. Competition is for industries — inside NEXUS, we only know collaboration.

🎯
Mission-Led

We build things that matter. Not just for exits, but for impact. Every startup in NEXUS must answer: what problem are you solving, and for whom?

💀
Fail Loudly

We celebrate public failure. Monthly "Ship It or Skip It" nights exist specifically so founders can post-mortem their failures — out loud, in front of everyone.

🧠
Always Learning

Curiosity is non-negotiable. Every member commits to one new skill per semester. We are perpetual students of business, design, tech, and people.
