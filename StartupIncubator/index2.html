// 03 — Program

12 weeks.
No shortcuts.

W1–2

Diagnostics & Alignment

Deep-dive sessions to map your business model, identify friction, and match you with your core mentor team.

W3–6

Traction Sprints

Weekly sprints with your mentor focused on one metric that matters. Ship fast, learn faster, kill what doesn't work.

W7–9

Capital Preparation

Narrative structuring, data room setup, and warm intros to investors who write checks at your stage.

W10–12

Launch & Demo

Public demo day, press blitz, and formal onboarding into the NEXUS alumni network and operator community.
